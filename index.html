CUNYSEC

A loose collective of CUNY students, alumni, industry professionals, and professors interested in information security and digital forensics.

Why did we start this?

The founding members saw an under representation of CUNY students and alumni in the security field. We plan to change that through collaboration and mentoring. CUNY has many talented students that could use a little direction. There is a skills shortage in information security. We are building the pipeline.

What do we do?

We attend security conferences and meetups, assist in networking students and alumni, and maintain an email list for: announcements, questions, and discussion. Occasionally, presentations are given by industry pros, alumni, and brave students.
